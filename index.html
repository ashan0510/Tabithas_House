Who We Are

Tabitha's House, Inc. is a 501(c)3 organization that provides housing, counseling, and intervention services to victims of sex trafficking.

What We Do

Tabitha's House provides resources to children, adolescents, and adults who are impacted by sex trafficking.

Why We Help

Tabitha's House aims to break the vicious cycle through awareness, prevention, identification, intervention, and education.

Volunteer

Volunteers are a part of our family. They help in almost all areas of Tabitha's House including prayer, our events, programs, community awareness, and fundraising. Tabitha's House could not provide high quality services without a supportive and involved community of volunteers who are motivated to change lives and enhance communities.

Become a part of our family. Together, we can save many lives!
